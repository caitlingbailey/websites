About me

My name is Caitlin, and I created this blog, Scratch Map Project. For some context to the name, I have a giant scratch map on my wall, and my “project” is that I want to be able to scratch off as much as possible!

After this new bout of exploration, I realised that I’d been in education non-stop for almost 18 years, wow! During my final year, I took advantage of the fact I had fewer hours to take some spontaneous trips. These included my first trip to Spain, and multiple trips to Slovenia. Being shown around Slovenia by a local meant I barely ran into any tourists, and learnt so much about the country in the process. In Barcelona, we scouted out some tucked away bars and restaurants, to find the most amazing food. So, I decided I wanted to continue this, and turned down a full-time Investment Banking job offer to go travelling. Sorry mum!
